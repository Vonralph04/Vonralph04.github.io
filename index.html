Cognitive Developmental Robotics Lab
International Research Center for Neurointelligence (IRCN) , The University of Tokyo
herbuela.vonralphdane@mail.u-tokyo.ac.jp

My research harnesses physiological signals and behavioral data to interpret cognitive and affective states, translating these insights into AI-driven systems using machine learning and advanced analytics. My goal is to support learning and communication for neurotypical and neurodiverse populations, and to study mother–infant interaction for tailored childcare support.
